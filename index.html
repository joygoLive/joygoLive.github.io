UQI — a vendor-neutral middleware for quantum computing

Bring your circuit once. UQI detects the framework, optimizes and noise-checks it, applies error mitigation, and runs it on the backend you choose — all through one consistent UI, API, and CLI. No vendor lock-in, no rewrites per provider.

How it works

One workflow — UI, API, and CLI in parity

Estimate cost, optimize, mitigate, and run with the same commands everywhere. Here's the command-line view.

uqi — zsh
$ uqi estimate --file bell.py
  ibm_fez   $0.00     iqm_garnet  $0.30
  rigetti   $0.74     ionq_forte  $82.22   # cheapest first

$ uqi catalog optimize --graph cycle6 --compare --resilience full
  CPU ✓ optimal      GPU(H100) ✓ optimal
  QPU(iqm_emerald)  fid 0.74 · valid 100%
  ↳ readout-mitigated  approx 100%   # post-processed, no re-run

Illustrative output. Costs are pre-submission estimates and vary by vendor and circuit.
